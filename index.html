20 Minute Meals

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 12oz spaghetti
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 8oz bacon, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated parmeggiano-reggiano
  • 1/2 cup grated pecorino romano

one

Bring large pot of water to a boil. Salt.

two

Heat oil and butter over medium heat. Sautee onion, garlic & bacon until onion is translucent.

three

Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.

Reserve 1/4 cup pasta water.

four

Beat eggs. Add cheese to eggs.

five

Drain pasta. Add to pan. Add 1tbsp of pasta water. Coat.

six

Remove pan from heat. Pour egg and cheese mixture into pan. Stir to coat.

Season liberally with black pepper. Serve immediately.
